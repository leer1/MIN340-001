What is something interesting and unique about you?

I am a full time storm chaser and hail researcher for THOR Storm Team that is in Bowling Green Kentucky. What I essentially do is collect and observe hail samples to collect data on hail size that will help further enhance our hail detecting technology in weather observations and forecasting. No, I have not seen a tornado, yet, but I have been chased down by one in Illinois in the dark, and I've mainly seen really large hail, the usual damaging winds and crazy storm structures.
